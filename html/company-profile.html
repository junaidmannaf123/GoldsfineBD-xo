About Goldsfine

Goldsfine International Ltd. is a global trading company dedicated to providing valuable business and supply chain service solutions through long-term relationships. Since our establishment in 2001, we have emphasized integrity, transparency, and accountability in our interactions with suppliers and customers.

We strive to earn and maintain their confidence and respect through our consistent efforts. With over 17 years of marketing expertise and in-depth knowledge of the paper, paperboard, packaging, pulp, waste paper, and related industries, we serve customers and suppliers worldwide by offering import, export, and various related services.

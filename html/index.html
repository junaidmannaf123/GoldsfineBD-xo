Our All Products

Medium Paper

High-quality corrugated fluting medium paper with excellent elasticity and tear resistance. Essential for corrugated box manufacturing.

Liner Paper

Virgin Kraft paper with superior strength, high tear resistance, and excellent bursting strength for corrugated carton boxes.

Duplex Board

Double-sided grey cardboard with coated glossy surface. Ideal for pharmaceutical packaging, food boxes, and premium applications.

Art Card/Paper

High-quality smooth paper with glossy finish, perfect for vibrant color reproduction and fine art printing applications.

Folding Box Board

Semi-rigid cardboard for high-end cosmetics, healthcare, pharmaceuticals, and premium packaging solutions.

Newsprint Paper

100% virgin fiber paper with ISO brightness level of 76. Perfect for newspapers, pamphlets, and publications.

Tapioca Starch

Apple Brand Starch from Thailand. Gluten-free and vegan flour substitute with excellent binding properties.

Waste Paper

Various grades of waste paper sourced globally. Includes OCC, ONP, and other recyclable paper products.

Pulp Paper

Bleached and unbleached hardwood and softwood pulp. Essential for paper manufacturing and recycling operations.
